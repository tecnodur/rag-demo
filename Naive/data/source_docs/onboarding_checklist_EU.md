# EU Employee Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist helps teams complete a consistent onboarding process for new EU hires. It covers contractual setup, payroll readiness, compliance training, device distribution, and account activation for core business systems.

## Pre-Start Tasks (Manager and HR)
Complete at least five business days before start date:
- Confirm signed employment contract and legal entity assignment
- Verify work location and payroll country
- Submit onboarding ticket with department, role, and cost center
- Assign onboarding buddy and first-week schedule
- Validate required hardware profile with IT Enablement

## Compliance and HR Setup
Required for all EU employees:
- Enroll employee in GDPR and data handling training
- Register employee in EU payroll and social contribution workflow
- Collect mandatory local tax and statutory information
- Provide privacy notices and records processing acknowledgment

## IT Provisioning and Access
IT Enablement should complete:
- Provision corporate identity account and mailbox
- Activate collaboration suite, HR portal, and service desk login
- Prepare laptop and endpoint security controls
- Assign baseline application access by role
- Share first-login instructions and authentication enrollment guide

## Password and Authentication Setup
During first login, the new hire must:
- Set initial password following the active company password policy
- Enroll in multifactor authentication for secure access
- Verify recovery contact methods for account support
- Test login to email, intranet, and one line-of-business application

Note: This checklist references account setup only. It is not the authoritative password policy document.

## Day One Validation
Manager and employee should verify:
- Device collection and endpoint compliance check completed
- Successful login and authentication to required systems
- Access to team channels, calendar, and shared resources
- Completion plan for remaining mandatory training

## Week One Completion
Finalize onboarding by end of week one:
- Confirm payroll record in "ready" status
- Confirm policy acknowledgments submitted
- Resolve access gaps through service requests
- Record checklist completion in HR operations tracker
